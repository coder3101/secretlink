Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

SecretLink is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your sensitive information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and what measures we take to protect it.

2. Data Collection and Storage

We collect and store only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Secret messages
  • Message expiration timestamps
  • Access logs (IP addresses and timestamps)

3. Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure storage of secret messages in our database
  • One-time access links that self-destruct after viewing
  • Automatic deletion of expired messages
  • Secure database storage with appropriate access controls

4. Data Usage

We use your data only for:

  • Providing the secret sharing service
  • Ensuring message delivery and access
  • Maintaining service security and preventing abuse
  • Improving our service quality

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary:

  • Secret messages are deleted immediately after being viewed
  • Expired messages are automatically removed
  • Access logs are retained for 30 days for security purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • File a complaint with data protection authorities

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact us at: privacy@coder3101.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
